FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What areas do you serve?

-

We proudly serve Long Island, the greater NYC area, and surrounding regions. Whether you need transportation within these areas or a specialized service, weve got you covered.

How do I book a ride?

+

Booking with RWP Trans Corp is easy! You can schedule a ride through our website or by calling our customer service hotline. We’re available to assist you with the details.

Is there assistance available for getting in and out of the vehicle?

+

Yes! Our trained drivers and vehicles are equipped to provide assistance, ensuring safe and comfortable boarding and exiting for all passengers, especially those with mobility challenges.

How far in advance should I book my ride?

+

We recommend booking your ride at least 24 to 48 hours in advance to ensure availability, but we do accommodate same-day bookings when possible.

Is your service available 24/7?

+

RWP Trans Corp provides transportation services seven days a week. However, we suggest checking availability for early morning or late-night rides in advance.
